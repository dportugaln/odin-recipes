Salmon Fillets

This is a salmon fillet

Fire up succulent blackened salmon with an exciting blend of Cajun-style spices!

Ingredients

These are the ingredients to make salmon fillets:

  • Ground paprika
  • Ground cayenne pepper
  • Salt
  • Ground white pepper
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dried thyme
  • Dried basil
  • Died oregano
  • Salmon fillets, skin and bones removed
  • Unsalted butter, melted

How to make salmon fillets:

Follow these directions to make salmon fillets:

  1. Mix spices
  2. Brush fillets with butter and sprinkle spices
  3. Cook fillets in butter until blackened
